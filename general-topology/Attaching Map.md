#definition
### Types
- `X` `Y` : Nonempty [[Topological Space]]
- `A` : [[Closed Set]] of `Y`
- $f : A \to X$
- f [[Continuity|Continuous]]
### Definition
If $X \cup_{f} Y$ is an [[Adjunction Space]], then it is said to be an *attaching* of $Y$ on $X$ along $f$, with $f$ being called the *attaching map*.

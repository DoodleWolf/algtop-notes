#definition
### Types
- `f` `g` : $\left[0, 1\right] \to X$, [[Path]]
### Definition
The *product path* $f \cdot g$ is a [[path]] defined by first going through $f$ then $g$. It is defined as using the formula:
$$
f \cdot g(s) =
\begin{cases}
f(2s), & 0 \leq s \leq \frac{1}{2} \\
g(2s - 1), & \frac{1}{2} \leq s \leq 1
\end{cases}
$$

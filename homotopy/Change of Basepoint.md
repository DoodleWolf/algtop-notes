#definition
### Types
- `X` : [[Topological Space]]
- $x_1 \space x_2$ : `X`
- `h` : [[Path]] from $x_1$ to $x_2$
### Definition
The map $\beta_h : \pi_1 \left(X, x_1\right) \to \pi_1 \left(X, x_2\right)$ defined as  $\beta_h\left[f\right] = \left[h \cdot f \cdot \bar h\right]$. It is an isomorphism of groups.
